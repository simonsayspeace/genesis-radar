# Prior Art Declaration — The Genesis Radar

## Declaration

This document formally establishes prior art for the multi-modal sensor fusion
architecture described in this repository.

## Inventor

- **GitHub:** simonsayspeace
- **Location:** Australia
- **Date of First Publication:** June 2026

## Original Contributions

The following specific combinations and implementations are claimed as original:

### 1. Multi-Modal Sensor Fusion for Through-Wall Biometric Identification

The combination of:
- UWB impulse radar (DW3000-based 5-module cross array)
- CSI WiFi channel state information mapping
- Facial recognition
- Voice recognition

Into a **single unified mathematical fusion algorithm** producing biometric
identification of individuals through solid walls without line of sight or
subject cooperation.

### 2. Foldable Cross-Array UWB Radar Device

A handheld through-wall radar device in a foldable cross/drone-frame
form factor with:
- 1 central transmitter
- 4 receivers at 40cm from centre on folding arms (Mavic Mini style hinges)
- Touchscreen display on rear face
- Vivaldi antenna blades recessed behind front face cutouts

### 3. Unified Dashboard

Real-time fusion display combining:
- Forward-facing X-ray style human blob visualisation
- Half-circle radar sweep
- Top-down plan view
- CSI WiFi avatar overlay
- Compass
- All driven from unified sensor fusion data

## What Is NOT Claimed

The following underlying technologies are prior art and not claimed:

- UWB ranging (Decawave/Qorvo prior art)
- DW3000 chip operation (Qorvo)
- CSI WiFi sensing (MIT, CMU prior art)
- Vivaldi antenna design (general prior art)
- Bayesian sensor fusion (general mathematical prior art)
- Individual facial/voice recognition systems

## What IS Claimed

The **specific combination and integration** of the above into a unified
through-wall biometric identification system on commodity hardware.
