⚠ Important Notes:
1. Place a 100nF decoupling capacitor between VCC and GND on every DW3000 module — as close to the chip as possible.
2. Keep SPI traces short and equal length — long unequal traces cause timing errors at high speed.
3. The 74HC138 active low outputs match the DW3000 CS pin perfectly — no inverter needed.
4. Use star ground — all GND wires meet at one central point to avoid ground loops.
5. Mount Vivaldi antennas parallel and facing forward — not angled or crossed.
